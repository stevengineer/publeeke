Get to know about Publekee

Publeeke is a web app that links brands with social media influencers.

It provides influencers with products to market and ads to circulate and earn. It provides brands a channel for influencer marketing.

Our solutions for your business growth

Through Publeeke, a publisher can add an advert and set relevant parameters such as the action payable either through share, click or other custom actions such as sale, filling a form, signing up, installing an app, watching a video), maximum payable actions and their cost, expiry date and redirect url.

An agent will get advertisement from Publeeke to share to his social pages and gets paid for the specified action A Publisher will then get analytics on his ad performance
